# Sandwich
Ingredients:
- 2 slices of bread
- 2 slices of cheese
- 2 slices of tomato
- 1 leaf of lettuce
- 1 tbsp mayonnaise

Instructions:
1. Spread mayonnaise on one side of each slice of bread.
2. Layer cheese, tomato, and lettuce on one slice.
3. Top with the other slice of bread.
4. Slice in half and enjoy!
